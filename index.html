Hollow Knight is peak!

In Hollow Knight, you will have fun exploring Hallownest, trekking through the empty Forgotten Crossroads, the lush greenery of Greenpath, and even the beautiful White Palace. Though, you will fight enemies and bosses like the easy False Knight , all the way to the painful Absolute Radiance. There is also platforming like the infamous Path of Pain. As soon as you unlock abilities like Dash and Mantis Claw, movement becomes much easier. Spells can also be upgraded: Vengeful Spirit → Shade Soul, Desolate Dive → Descending Dark, and Howling Wraiths → Abyss Shriek.
